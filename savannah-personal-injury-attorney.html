Medical Malpractice

Medical malpractice cases present some of the most complicated legal claims. A doctor, hospital, or medical provider has a duty to provide appropriate care to all patients. When medical care falls short of established standards, victims have the right to file a malpractice claim against their medical provider. There are very strict legal requirements for asserting a medical malpractice claim. Your claim must be filed within a specified period of time after the incident occurred. Your lawsuit must also have an accompanying affidavit from a professional witness certifying that, in their opinion, you received substandard care. Our Savannah medical malpractice attorneys can help when you or a loved one suffered injuries as a result of improper medical care.

Premises Liability

Premises liability laws are in place to keep visitors safe. Property owners have a duty to ensure that their premises are safe for all lawful visitors or guests. When an invited visitor or guest sustains injuries on a property, the property owner may be legally responsible for the injuries. In cases where the owner has failed to maintain the premises in a reasonably safe condition, an injured victim may recover damages for any injuries that they have suffered.
