The term negligence refers to conduct by a person that is determined to be carelessness that caused damage or harm to another individual. There is common cause as well. Statute of Limitations in Savannah GA

This refers to the amount of time that an individual has to file a lawsuit or claim on their vehicle accident. The statute of limitations is applicable to all personal injury cases that involve injuries that are filed in the civil court system of Georgia. Georgia's statute of limitations is two years for vehicle accidents. The time starts on the date that the individual's accident occurred or when an injury is experienced.

Following A Car Accident in Savannah

Whenever an individual is involved in a car accident, they tend to panic quite often and are not sure what they should do next. With emotions running very high, it can be a big struggle to remember everything that should be done. You should stay at the scene of the accident and not attempt to leave. You should also check yourself for injuries and other people, if possible. If necessary call 9-1-1 if you can. Gather contact information from all of the people who were involved in the car accident along with any potential witnesses. Take photos to help with a potential insurance claim in the future. Your next step is to file a claim with the insurance company and call an experienced lawyer to discuss your case details.
