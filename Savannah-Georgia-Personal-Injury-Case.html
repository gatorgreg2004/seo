Who Can I Hold Liable in Case of a Savannah Personal Injury Accident?

If you sustain injuries after a slip and fall accident, you can sue the party in whose premises you sustained the accident. The damages for slip and fall accidents are typically compensated using the liability insurance owned by the at-fault party. This means that on lodging a slip and fall claim, you will be dealing with the insurance company that covers the at-fault party.

It is the business of insurance companies to always deny any liability on the part of their clients so as no to pay any compensation for personal injury claims. To file a successful case after a slip and fall accident, you have to prove that the at-fault party was negligent and they did not provide safe conditions and as such, your accident was unavoidable.

Proving this fault can be difficult if you do not have a talented and highly skilled attorney looking after your legal interests. How to Hire a Savannah Personal Injury Law Firm

Though it is not necessary, immediately after your slip and fall accident, you should inform the at-fault party of the incident and make it known that you will be filing a personal injury claim. It is important to let the at-fault party know of the incident so that they can create a record. If you do not inform the at-fault party when the accident happens, they can end up denying the occurrence of the accident when you bring it up later or even claim that you are altering the facts on what actually happened.

Since the insurance company for the at-fault party will be fighting hard to prove that their client was not negligent, you need to collect proof and documentation proving that indeed the at-fault party was negligent. On contacting our lawyers, we will advise you all the documentation and information you need to formulate a formidable claim case.

Some of the documents that can help bolster your case include:

• Videos or photos of the scene of the slip and fall accident (It is best that you take photos and videos of the accident scene as soon as it happens)
